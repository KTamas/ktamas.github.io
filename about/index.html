    About Just a Theory

    Just a Theory is an periodically irregular technology and culture blog by David E. Wheeler. If you’d like to get in touch, catch me on Twitter or send email to david at this domain.

    Colophon

    Just a Theory is written in Markdown, built and published with Hugo, and hosted on AWS. Its set in Source Sans Pro, with fixed-width type set in Source Code Pro. The icons are by Font Awesome.

    This site eschews JavaScript, and uses no tracking or analytics services.
